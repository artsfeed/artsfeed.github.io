Arts Feed Staff The people behind the art appreciation

David Shin Founder / Editor in Chief / Oboist

Prior to joining ArtsFeed, he was appointed the editorial editor for the Science Survey and the student life reporter for the Observatory. Besides journalism, David is an active member of the arts community and is an oboist of various youth ensembles including Carnegie Hall’s Future Music Project Ensemble and the precollege division of the Manhattan School of Music. He is inspired by David Remnick, the editor-in-chief of the New Yorker magazine, and The New York Times columnist Nicholas Kristof. In college, he hopes to study music, communications, and management. Chamber

Shafqat Shadaab Founder / Business Manager

Outside of ArtsFeed, Shafqat is the Co-President of the Bronx Science Business Society and an active member in his community. Shafqat hopes to major in economics and acquire a career in the financial world. In ArtsFeed, Shafqat integrates his passion for business with his love for writing as the magazine’s Business Manager.

Boubacar Diallo Technology Manager / Site Designer

Vice President of Bronx Science BOSS/WIS club and aspiring programmer, Boubacar designed and manages this site.
