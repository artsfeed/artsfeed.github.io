David Shin Founder / Editor in Chief / Oboist

David Shin is the editor-in-chief of ArtsFeed. Prior to joining ArtsFeed, he was appointed the editorial editor for the Science Survey and the student life reporter for the Observatory. Besides journalism, David is an active member of the arts community and is an oboist of various youth ensembles including Carnegie Hall’s Future Music Project Ensemble and the precollege division of the Manhattan School of Music. He is inspired by David Remnick, the editor-in-chief of the New Yorker magazine, and The New York Times columnist Nicholas Kristof. In college, he hopes to study music, communications, and management. Chamber

Shafqat Shadaab Founder / Business Manager

Shafqat Shadaab is the business manager of ArtsFeed Magazine. He is currently a senior at the Bronx High School of Science, and hopes to attend college in the fall of 2017. Outside of ArtsFeed, Shafqat is the Co-President of the Bronx Science Business Society and an active member in his community. Shafqat hopes to major in economics and acquire a career in the financial world. In ArtsFeed, Shafqat integrates his passion for business with his love for writing as the magazine’s Business Manager.
