MISSION

ArtsFeed is the official print and online arts publication of the Bronx High School of Science. As a publication, it provides a platform for students to discuss, write, and depict art in an independent medium. The content encompasses a broad range of arts involving drama, dance, music, pop culture, and more. Contributors are encouraged to convey their unfiltered thoughts in whatever form they desire. This medium serves as a place where we see the art in the world, from politics to music.

We believe everyone wants a voice. Throughout history, our voices have been shunned. But there have always been those who spoke up for those who could not express-the voices of the voiceless. Those voices, although spanning many generations and covering many diverse topics, had one thing in common: art.
