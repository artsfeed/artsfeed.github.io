Founder / Business Manager

At Fordham, Shafqat is the Freshman Marketing Coordinator of the Commuting Students Association and a part of the Asian Cultural Exchange club. He was the Co-President of the Bronx Science Business Society. Shafqat hopes to major in business administration with concentrations in marketing and management. David Shin

Founder / Editor in Chief / Oboist

Prior to founding ArtsFeed, he was appointed the editorial editor for the Science Survey and the senior student life reporter for the Observatory. Besides journalism, David is an active member of the arts community and is an oboist for various youth ensembles including the New York Youth Symphony and the precollege division of the Manhattan School of Music. He is inspired by David Remnick, the editor-in-chief of the New Yorker magazine, and The New York Times columnist Nicholas Kristof. In college, he hopes to study music, communications, and business management. Chamber
